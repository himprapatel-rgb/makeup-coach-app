# Research

This folder contains all research documentation for the Use-What-You-Have Makeup Coach project.

## Research Categories

### 1. Market Research
- Competitive analysis
- Target audience analysis
- Market trends and opportunities

### 2. Problem Statement Research
- User pain points
- Current market gaps
- Technical barriers

### 3. Technical Research
- AI/ML technologies
- Face detection and analysis
- Ingredient databases and APIs

### 4. User Research
- User personas
- User journey mapping
- Survey and interview findings

### 5. Regulatory & Compliance Research
- Privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Beauty industry standards
- AI ethics considerations

## How to Use This Folder

Each research category will have its own subfolder with relevant documents, findings, and references.
