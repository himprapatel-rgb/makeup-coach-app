## 4. Market Gaps & Opportunities

### 4.1 Critical Unmet Needs

Based on our analysis, the following gaps represent significant opportunities:

#### Gap 1: Skill-Building Over Sales
- **Problem:** 75% of beauty buyers own unused products due to difficulty replicating tutorials
- **Current Apps:** Focus on virtual try-on and commerce, not teaching application techniques
- **Opportunity:** Coach users to master techniques with products they already own

#### Gap 2: Personalized Learning Paths
- **Problem:** Generic recommendations that don't adapt to user progress
- **Current Apps:** One-size-fits-all tutorials and suggestions
- **Opportunity:** AI-driven personalized skill development with progress tracking

#### Gap 3: Inclusive Representation
- **Problem:** 53% of Black consumers report difficulty finding matching products
- **Current Apps:** AI often performs poorly on darker skin tones, older faces, non-Western features
- **Opportunity:** Truly inclusive AI trained on diverse datasets

#### Gap 4: Product Safety & Ingredient Intelligence
- **Problem:** Generic good/bad ingredient scores without personalization
- **Current Apps:** Fragmented ingredient information, no cross-checking with user sensitivities
- **Opportunity:** Personalized ingredient analysis based on skin type and concerns

#### Gap 5: Closed Learning Loop
- **Problem:** No way to track improvement or learn from mistakes
- **Current Apps:** Disconnected before/after tracking, no feedback mechanisms
- **Opportunity:** Progress tracking, expert feedback, skill milestones
